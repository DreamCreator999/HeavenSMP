About the Server

Survival Focus

We believe in the core Minecraft experience. Build, explore, and survive together in a pure environment without heavy modifications.

Community Driven

Heaven Craft is built on trust and collaboration. Establish towns, trade with neighbors, and participate in server-wide events.

Server Rules

01

No Griefing or Stealing: Respect other players' builds and items. Unauthorized destruction will result in a ban.

02

PVP is Opt-In: Do not attack other players unless both parties have explicitly agreed to a duel.

03

No Cheating: The use of x-ray texture packs, hacked clients, or exploits is strictly prohibited.

04

Be Respectful: Keep the chat clean and respectful. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated.
